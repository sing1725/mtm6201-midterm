Our Menu

Bubble Tea

Satisfy your cravings with our refreshing Bubble Tea, made with your choice of premium tea, sweetened with syrup, and topped with chewy tapioca pearls. A unique beverage experience that's sure to please your taste buds.

Coffee

Experience the unique taste of our Japanese coffee, made from carefully selected beans, and brewed with precision. Enjoy a rich and smooth flavor, with hints of chocolate and nutty undertones. Perfect for coffee aficionados who appreciate the art and science of coffee-making.

Souffle Cake

Indulge in our delicate Souffle Cake, made with the finest ingredients, and baked to perfection. Light and airy, with a melt-in-your-mouth texture, it's the perfect dessert to satisfy your sweet tooth. Try it today and taste the difference!

Sponge Cake

Our Sponge Cake is a classic dessert that's both light and fluffy. Made with simple yet high-quality ingredients, it's perfect for any occasion. Enjoy it on its own or topped with your favorite fruits and cream for a delightful treat.

Sushi

Indulge in our fresh and delicious sushi, a Japanese delicacy that's perfect for any meal. Our expertly crafted rolls feature the finest quality seafood, crisp vegetables, and perfectly seasoned rice, all wrapped in delicate seaweed. Experience the perfect combination of flavors and textures in every bite.

Gourmet Meal

Treat yourself to a gourmet Japanese meal, featuring the best of Japanese cuisine. Our menu boasts a variety of delicious dishes made with premium ingredients, including fresh sushi, savory tempura, and flavorful ramen. Experience the art of Japanese cooking with every bite and savor a culinary journey like no other.
